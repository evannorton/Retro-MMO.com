Version History

v1.12.0 - 2025-09-19

  • Improved the icons for the "Impale" ability and "Bleed" and "Poison" statuses.
  • Improved the animation for the "Impale" ability.
  • Fixed a bug which could cause battlers to do their action after dying from bleed or poison.
  • Fixed a bug which could cause incorrect inventory and stats to be displayed.
  • Fixed a bug which could cause equipment level requirements to not be displayed.
  • Fixed a bug which could cause music and sound effects to play at incorrect volumes.

v1.11.1 - 2025-09-06

  • Fixed retargeted attacks in battle to prioritize monsters of the same type.
  • Fixed bugs which caused targetting numbers to not render properly outside of battle.
  • Fixed incorrect position of battle animations and status icons for certain monsters.

v1.11.0 - 2025-09-06

  • Implemented a new "Focus" resource pool for the Warrior class.
  • Added a new "Impale" ability for the Warrior class.
  • Added a new "Poison" ability for the Cleric class.
  • Changed "Guard" ability's level requirement to 4.
  • Added music to the character select screen.
  • Fixed a bug which caused the battle round timer to take effect even in solo combat.
  • Fixed a bug which allowed you to play a piano from the sides or behind.

v1.10.0 - 2025-08-20

  • Changed the color of the roof on Maika's house.
  • Reduced the likelihood of ambushes in battle.
  • Fixed many bugs with battle.

v1.9.0 - 2025-04-06

  • Removed the Wizard Girl Dress-Up! integration.
  • Fixed a bug which prevented quests from functioning properly.

v1.8.0 - 2025-04-04

  • Adjusted the layout of the inn.
  • Added a profanity filter for chat.
  • Fixed bugs related to inviting players to party, battle and trade.

v1.7.0 - 2025-03-01

  • Added a display that shows experience gained and gold earned upon completing a quest.

v1.6.1 - 2025-01-09

  • Fixed a bug which could prevent players from properly rendering on the map.
  • Fixed a bug which could cause the Vitality ability's health regeneration to not properly render.
  • Fixed multiple bugs related to party/trade/duel invite indicators.
  • Removed the fourth birthday celebration.
